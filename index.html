About Me

From atmospheric science to AI-powered software.

I am Joseph Njeri, a full-stack software developer and data scientist based in Karlsruhe, Germany. My background combines machine learning, data engineering, and modern web development, allowing me to create complete end-to-end solutions.

My academic background is in atmospheric science. I hold a Doctor of Natural Sciences from the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, where I researched the diagnosis and simulation of extreme precipitation events over Kenya.

I have professional experience in predictive modeling, recommender systems, image recognition, financial modeling, API development, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Django REST Framework, Flask, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.

I am open to collaborations and opportunities at the intersection of data science, AI, business intelligence, and full-stack software development.
